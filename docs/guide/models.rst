ModelStructure
--------------

In PyDataColl there are three type of model: **DEVICE**, **TERM**, **ITEM**. their relationship can be shown as this:

Device1:
    ->Term1:
        ->Item1
        ->Item2
        ->...
    ->Term2:
        ->Item1
        ->...
    ->...
Device2:
    ->Term3
        ...
